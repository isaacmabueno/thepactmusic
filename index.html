About

The Pact

Over the past 9 months, The Pact has been developing their craft at SonWaves Recording Studio and playing shows around the city. Although they have been performing their new material for less than a year, they have been fortunate enough to play iconic venues like House Of Blues, Bottom Lounge, Beat Kitchen and Empty Bottle and share the stage with great acts such as Half/Noise, Mike Mains & The Branches, Vinyl Theater, Spirit Animal, Tribe Society and many more.

All of this hard work has lead to the release of their new EP “Reaching In The Dark”. This genre blending 5 song release perfectly blends indie rock melodies with rock driven rhythms, showing how much the band has grown and where they are headed. "Reaching In The Dark" is now available everywhere digitally and physically at Lincoln Hall on February 27th, as they share the stage with Family & Friends. For a more in depth look into the band, check out their recent feature on Listen Live and Local. Enjoy.
