About

The Pact

is an Indie/Alternative Rock band from Chicago, IL that formed in 2012. The band's first EP, “Insignificant,” was released in December of 2012 and after many changes and transitions, The Pact is ready to debut their new material, sound and complete member line up to the public. Over the past six months, The Pact has been developing their craft in Caleb’s home studio and playing shows around the city. Even though they have been playing live with their new material for less than a year, they have been fortunate enough to share with great acts such as: Half/Noise, Mike Mains & The Branches, Mighty Fox, Spirit Animal, Vinyl Theater and many more. The Pact may be “Reaching In The Dark” right now, but the future is looking very bright for this talented group of musicians.
